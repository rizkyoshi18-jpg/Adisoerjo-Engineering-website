About Us

CV. Adisoerjo Engineering

Established in 2011, CV. Adisoerjo Engineering is a leading civil engineering consulting firm based in Indonesia. We specialize in providing comprehensive solutions for infrastructure, construction, and environmental projects. Our team of experienced engineers delivers high-quality designs, project management, and technical consulting to ensure successful outcomes for our clients.

With a commitment to innovation, sustainability, and client satisfaction, we have successfully completed numerous projects ranging from structural designs to photovoltaic installations and control systems.

  • Experience: Over 50 projects completed
  • Mission: Building a better future through engineering excellence
